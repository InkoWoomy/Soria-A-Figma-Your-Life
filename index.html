About Alexander Soria


Alexander Soria is a person whose always got his nose in his computer, and no one can seem to get him away from it.

He enjoys video games down to everything they offer, from how they play, the music and atmosphere, as well as their stories. The inspiration is what fuels him to learn how to make his very own video game.

In his off time, he does digital art, usually of the Splatoon variety, both of the traditional and digital formats of art. He describes his sketches as “chicken scratch.”

He also works part-time in fast food.
